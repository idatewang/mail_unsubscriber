Welcome to The University of Texas at Austin!

Thank you for registering for the New Graduate Student Orientation. This event is in addition to your department orientation.

Below you will find information on what you will need for the day of orientation. We are excited to meet you!
Please make sure to either print out your Eventbrite ticket and QR code or have it ready on your phone. You can download the Eventbrite app for iOS or Android. We will be scanning your ticket to check you in. Join us in the Santa Rita Suite in the Union for breakfast starting at 8 am.
If you have not done so already, download our UT Orientation app where you can create your own schedule based on what interests you. You can also view the schedule on this website.
You now have access to the Graduate School Orientation Canvas page that will provide you with videos to help you prepare for your time at UT. Log in to Canvas and enroll after completing your pre-arrival checklist.

GSA Social | Aug. 19 | 5 - 8 pm | PCL Scholar Commons

This family friendly event will allow graduate students to meet other new graduate students from across campus. This event will be held in the Perry-Casteñada Library in the Scholar Commons located on the ground floor.
On Wednesday, August 24, President Jay Hartzell will officially welcome you to the Longhorn community with a unique celebration,Gone to Texas 2022. This event is a rich tradition for all new students and formally marks your start at the university. Gone to Texas 2022 will take place at 8:30 p.m. on the Main Mall, with a webcast available for those not able to attend in person.
Welcome to the University of Texas at Austin.

We look forward to seeing you on Friday!
