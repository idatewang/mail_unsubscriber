Hi there,

We're so happy you signed up for Notion! We've packed a lot of unusual features into this tool to help you take better notes and feel more organized. To get you started, here's a quick walkthrough.

We recommend opening a blank Notion page to try things as you go 😉

Go to Notion
 

(1) First, just start typing...

Create a new page and just type. Notion can be this simple. Notice how all the features fade away? This is designed to help you focus on your ideas 💭

(2) Add many different types of content 🏗

It's not just about text. Notion makes it easy to add headings, checklists, images, videos, tables, embeds, and more. Just type / to see the full menu!

 

(3) Drag and drop to rearrange anything 🔥🔥🔥

This is one of the most special things about Notion. Extremely useful for organizing your thoughts on the fly.
 

(4) Organize pages inside pages 📄→ 📄

No more messy folders. Notion helps you put everything in its place by nesting pages inside pages. Add infinite layers of info, and find it all in your sidebar!

(5) Try a database 🔍

Notion gets even more powerful with databases. Add a table, calendar, or Kanban board to organize your work in more ways!
