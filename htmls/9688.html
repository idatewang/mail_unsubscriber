Three ways to share your projects:

1) Kickoff your project with email sharing
Share a project by email to start work faster.  Identify issues, constraints, and opportunities right on the map!

Give it a try:
  1. Click Share in the top right menu
  2. Enter the email of the project collaborator 
  3. Decide permission level (viewer, editor)
  4. Send the invite! 
Learn More

2) Link sharing for even faster stakeholder collaboration

Design projects involve many stakeholders - get everyone on the same page with one-click link sharing.  Create a project link that anyone can access from anywhere at any time - you control access and permissions.  

Try it now: 

  1. Click Share in the top right menu
  2. Look for Share by Link
  3. Copy the link and share with collaborators 
Learn More

3) Avoid sending hundreds of emails, use real-time sharing instead! 
Miscommunication or misaligned objectives can cause months of delay in design projects. Work together in real time in Equator and create a central source of truth for project discussions.

How it works: 
  • Share a project using method 1 or 2 (above)
  • Team members join the project at the same time
  • Interact with the map together!
Don't worry about saving - all of your changes are saved automatically!
