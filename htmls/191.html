Dear Duo,

Recent developments in mRNA technology may lead to flu vaccines that could be developed more quickly and may be better matched to each season’s specific strains.

If you haven’t had a flu vaccine in the last six months, you could be eligible to participate in a research study for an investigational mRNA vaccine that may protect against the flu virus.

The mRNA Flu Vaccine Study will help researchers learn if this investigational vaccine is safe and can help the body produce antibodies, which may help fight off the flu.
