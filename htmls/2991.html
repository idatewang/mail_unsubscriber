Mobility Week 2022

Making Transport More Sustainable

Dear Duo Wang,

A growing need to move people and goods in a more sustainable way is driving the development of innovative solutions towards making transportation cleaner, faster, safer and more comfortable.

For the future of transportation to become truly sustainable and accessible to all, professionals from various disciplines need to be prepared to adopt, implement and advance these developments.

In the context of the European Mobility Week, we at TU Delft wish to reaffirm our commitment and contribution to these goals by moving ideas and knowledge all around the world.

Our online courses in this field will help you lead the way, enhance your skill set and improve your job prospects. Check out some of our upcoming courses listed below or read more about our portfolio of courses for sustainable transport.

Join us and contribute to the future of mobility!
