What exactly is RPA?

Robotic process automation (RPA) software allows for the automation of routine computer tasks through a set of predefined rules. 

Where are the advantages?

Automating routine tasks with robots saves time and effort, increasing business productivity, adaptability, and responsiveness. The elimination of everyday tasks boosts employee happiness, engagement, and output.

What makes RPA so revolutionary?

Software robots replace humans in repetitive or low-value tasks, such as accessing and navigating different systems and applications, reorganizing files and folders, extracting, copying, and pasting data, completing and submitting forms, and generating reports. Cognitive tasks, such as text interpretation, chat and conversation participation, understanding of unstructured data, and the application of sophisticated machine learning models to the making of complex decisions, are now within the capabilities of the most advanced robots.
